September 10, 2010

There were 11 submissions to this contest. All submissions including videos and additional images can be downloaded here. (~ 550 MB).

For each paper, three reviews have been made by visualization experts. For the best papers (upper 80%), two additional reviews have been done by neurosurgical domain experts (see committee for details).

 

The winning submission and three honorable-mention submissions will be announced at IEEE Vis 2010. Congratulations to the winner and the three honorable-mention submissions:
